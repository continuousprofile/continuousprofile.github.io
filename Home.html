The Continuous Profile® is a 3-dimensional rendering where the front portion of the human head in profile is used as a pattern for turning on a wood lathe, potters wheel, or rendered for display as an animated virtual image. The resulting object is an abstraction of the facial profile that is identical throughout the entire 360 degrees when rotated on the vertical axis.  Similar to snowflakes and fingerprints, it is believed that no two human profiles are exactly the same; therefore all Continuous Profiles® are unique, yielding infinite variety.
