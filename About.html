The Continuous Profile is an interactive eBook based on the sculpture “Profilo Continuo del Duce” by Italian artist Renato Bertelli.   Using interactive animations and other multi-media widgets, the eBook describes the processes used to electronically render unique Continuous Profiles of your family, friends, and even pets.  This Craft eBook provides guidance to create a unique art abstraction using your favorite software to design and create a 3D rendering on your Mac® or PC.
