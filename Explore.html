So what is this all about? 


I was intrigued with the concept of The Continuous Profile® when I first saw a picture of Profilo Continuo del Duce (Continuous profile of Mussolini) by Italian artist Renato Bertelli.  I wondered what other profiles might look like if subjected to the same process.  Being a woodworker, I decided to find out for myself and set out to terrorize family and friends with my abstract creations of them.   Samples of the wood objects can be seen in the Gallery page.


With a background in computers, it was natural for me to explore the concept in digital form, so I started creating 3-D renderings as seen in the Gallery page.  Now the quest is to see if CNC can be used to automate the manufacture.  For better or worse, the idea of The Continuous Profile™ has exposed me to some really interesting technologies and hours of fun, even though the concept is rather simple (perhaps even trivial?).  Is it Art?  OpArt?  PopArt?  Who knows!!!
